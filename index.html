Privacy Policy for Islam Pro (إسلام برو)

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Islam Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting your spiritual privacy while providing a high-quality Islamic experience. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your data when you use the Islam Pro application.

1. Location Data (Precise & Approximate)

We require your location to provide accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, and to find nearby mosques. This data is processed locally on your device or via temporary API calls, and is never shared or sold to third parties.

2. User Account Information

We collect your email address and name if you choose to sign in with Google or create an account. This data is used solely for cloud synchronization of your bookmarks, settings, and religious progress via Google Firebase.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time directly through the Account Settings in the app. Upon deletion, all associated information is immediately and permanently purged from our servers.

4. Security

All data transmissions between the application and our servers are secured using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.
